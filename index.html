Currently Working:

Quality Assurance Engineer at RidewithGPS.com.

I've been working at RWGPS since 2014. I started as a Customer Service rep then quickly started taking care of a lot of other tasks like simple product photography, shipping, writing help articles, recording training videos for our users, feature advocacy, custom large format maps for printing, readying material for print, and for the last few years I've been focusing on Quality Assurance for the mobile and web teams.
